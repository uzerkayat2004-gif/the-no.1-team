# Consensus Rules

## Voting
- After Round 3 of brainstorm, Senior Agent counts votes
- Each agent votes: **Agree** or **Disagree**
- **3-1 majority** → point is resolved, majority wins
- **2-2 tie** → escalate to Boss for final decision
- **Unanimous** → point is confirmed with high confidence

## Minority Reports
- ALWAYS recorded when consensus is reached with dissent
- The dissenting agent must document:
  - Their final position
  - Their evidence
  - Why they disagree with the majority
- Stored in session history for future reference

## Tie-Breaking
- Boss reviews both sides
- Boss can request quick targeted research on the disputed point
- Boss makes final call — no further debate after Boss decides
