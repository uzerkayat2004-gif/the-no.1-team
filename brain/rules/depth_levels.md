# Research Depth Levels

## Level 1 — Quick Scan
- **Brainstorm:** ❌ Skip (direct combine, bullet output)
- **Boss Interrupts:** ❌ (notified on failures only)
- **Use when:** Simple fact-finding, quick answers, low-stakes questions
- **Expected time:** Minutes
- **Output:** Bullet points, no synthesis doc

## Level 2 — Mid-Level (Default)
- **Brainstorm:** ✅ Full 3-round format
- **Boss Interrupts:** ✅ Allowed
- **Use when:** Standard research, feature comparisons, analysis
- **Expected time:** 10-30 minutes
- **Output:** Full synthesis doc with citations

## Level 3 — Deep Dive
- **Brainstorm:** ✅ Full 3-round format
- **Boss Interrupts:** ✅ Allowed
- **Use when:** Critical decisions, competitive analysis, architecture design
- **Expected time:** 30-60 minutes
- **Output:** Comprehensive report with multiple perspectives

## Level 4 — Maximum
- **Brainstorm:** ✅ Extended (additional rounds if needed)
- **Boss Interrupts:** ✅ Allowed
- **Use when:** Mission-critical research, legal/compliance, security audits
- **Expected time:** 1+ hours
- **Output:** Exhaustive report, all minority positions documented
