# Red Team Rules

## Assignment
- Auto-assigned to the agent with **lowest performance score**
- Red Team ≠ Senior Agent (always different agents)
- Boss can take Red Team role personally
- Rotates as performance scores change

## Responsibilities
1. **Challenge every major point** with specific evidence or counter-arguments
2. Challenge even points you personally agree with — the goal is stress-testing
3. Must provide **counter-evidence**, not just "I disagree"
4. Look for:
   - Weak sources
   - Logical fallacies
   - Missing perspectives
   - Outdated information
   - Confirmation bias

## Format
When challenging a point:
```
🎯 CHALLENGE: [point being challenged]
Counter-evidence: [specific source or reasoning]
Risk if we proceed: [what could go wrong]
Recommendation: [alternative approach]
```
