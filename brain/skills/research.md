# Skill: Research

## How To Research At Each Depth Level

### Level 1 — Quick Scan
- Bullet points only
- 2-3 key findings
- No deep analysis
- Speed over depth

### Level 2 — Mid-Level (Default)
- Standard research with sources
- 5-10 key findings
- Basic analysis and comparison
- Confidence score on each finding

### Level 3 — Deep Dive
- Thorough multi-source research
- 10-20 findings with detailed analysis
- Cross-reference sources
- Identify contradictions and gaps
- Provide recommendations

### Level 4 — Maximum
- Exhaustive research
- Leave nothing unresearched
- Academic-level depth
- Historical context + future predictions
- Compare competing viewpoints in detail

## Research Format
```
## Finding: [topic]
**Source:** [where you found this]
**Summary:** [what you found]
**Confidence:** X/10
**Notes:** [any caveats or concerns]
```

## Rules
- Research INDEPENDENTLY during Phase 2
- Do NOT reference other agents' work
- Do NOT use past session data unless Boss says "check past research"
- Past data must be marked `[FROM PAST SESSION]`
